Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

True Conext LLC ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share personal data when working with creators, brands, and business partners.

1. What We Collect

  • Names, social media profiles, and contact information
  • Documents such as IDs, signed contracts, rate sheets
  • Private quotes, availability, and general location for campaign planning

2. How We Use the Data

We use the information to:

  • Coordinate brand campaigns with creators
  • Maintain records for business and legal purposes
  • Communicate project timelines and requirements

3. Sharing

We share select data with trusted clients and brand partners as part of campaign coordination, including:

  • Social metrics and audience info
  • Rates, availability, and general location

4. Your Rights

You may request to access, modify, or delete your personal information by emailing us at admin@trueconext.com.

5. Security

We store sensitive documents securely. While no system is 100% secure, we implement industry-standard practices to protect data.

6. International Users

True Conext is a Wyoming-registered LLC. Your data may be accessed or stored in the United States, and by using our services, you consent to this transfer.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Updates will be posted here with a new effective date.

8. Contact

For questions or concerns, contact us at admin@trueconext.com.
